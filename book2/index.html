💡Hair Fun Facts

  • The average person has 100,000–150,000 strands of hair.
  • Hair is made of keratin, the same protein found in nails.
  • The fastest-growing tissue in the human body (after bone marrow) is hair.
  • Red hair is the rarest color (only 1–2% of the world’s population).
  • A single strand of hair can support up to 100 grams of weight.
